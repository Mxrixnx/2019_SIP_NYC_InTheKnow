Police Brutality

What is Police Brutality?

Police Brutality is something that happens all around the world. It is the use of excessive and/or unnecessary force by police when dealing with civilians. Police Brutality is not somethng that is just physical. It can also take many forms such as false arrests, verbal abuse, sexual abuse and so much more.
