Police Brutality

What is Police Brutality?

Police Brutality is something that happens all around the world. It is the use of excessive and/or unnecessary force by police when dealing with civilians. Police Brutality is not somethng that is just physical. It can also take many forms such as false arrests, verbal abuse, sexual abuse and so much more.

What Can You Do To Help?

When it comes to stoping police brutality, there is not much that you can do on your own, but with the help of your community you will be able to make a huge change in the system.Something as small as letting other people know about police brutality can help to get evryone in your community involved in peaceful protests and can let people know that it is ok to speak up when we see police officers being overly aggresive.Also make sure to wear a body camera as much as possible so that if a police officer does anything to you, it is possible for you to take the police officer to authorities and
