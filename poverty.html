What does it mean to experience poverty?

Families and their children experience poverty when they are unable to achieve a minimum, decent standard of living that allows them to participate fully in mainstream society.These non-material resources provide families with the means to get by, and ultimately, to get ahead.Poverty also involves homeless people and hunger.
